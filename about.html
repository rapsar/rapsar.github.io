about

Call me Raphaël. I am a physicist and naturalist based in Colorado, with ties in New England and Europe. Some years ago, I discovered the wondrous world of fireflies and it has become a passion. Beyond their scientific interest and poetic character, I have also realized that fireflies connect people together and define some aspects of our relationship to nature and the living world.
