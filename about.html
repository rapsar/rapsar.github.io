about

Call me Raphaël. I am a physicist and naturalist based in Colorado. Some years ago, I discovered the wondrous world of fireflies and they have since become a persistent pursuit. Beyond their scientific interest and poetic character, I have realized that fireflies also connect people together and animate our relationship to nature and wildlife.
